MASTERTHESIS – Change Management in remote SME

Small and medium sized companies that are growing in size are affected stronger by the addition of new epmloyees to the workforce than large companies. The challenge in remotely operated companies is to communicate the internal culture and way of thinking.

In my masters thesis, I explored the ways three remote companies that have between 30 and 80 employees how they approach the challenge of Change Management in a remote setting.

One of the key take-aways is the insight that Change Management as it classically is taught doesn’t apply to highly digitized companies. So, are OKRs and other goal setting frameworks the better option for a contemporary Change Management?
